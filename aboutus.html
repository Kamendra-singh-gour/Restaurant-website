About Us



Our History

Started in 2020,Food for Foodies quickly established itself as a culinary icon par excellence in law gate,Punjab. With its unique brand of fusion cuisine that can be found nowhere else, it enjoys patronage from the A-list clientele in Vertos. Featuring four of the best chefs.

The restaurant traces its humble beginnings to The Frying Pan, a successful chain started by our CEO, Mr. Kamendra Singh, that featured for the first time the world's best cuisines in a pan.





Corporate Leadership



Vinay Chauhan Chief Epicurious Officer

Our CEO, vinay, credits his hardworking North indian immigrant parents who undertook the arduous journey to the shores of india with the intention of giving their children the best future. His mother's Vandhan in the kitchen whipping up the tastiest dishes with whatever is available inexpensively at the supermarket, was his first inspiration to create the fusion cuisines for which The Frying Pan became well known. He brings his zeal for fusion cuisines to this restaurant, pioneering cross-cultural culinary connections.

Sushant Bharti Mohan Chief Food Officer

Our CFO, Sushant, as he is affectionately referred to by his colleagues, comes from a long established family tradition in farming and produce. His experiences growing up on a farm in the indian-Himachali outback gave him great appreciation for varieties of food sources. As he puts it in his own words, Everything that runs, wins, and everything that stays, pays!

Vyapak Mishra Chief Taste Officer

Blessed with the most discerning gustatory sense, Vyapak Mishra, our CTO, personally ensures that every dish that we serve meets his exacting tastes. Our chefs dread the tongue lashing that ensues if their dish does not meet his exacting standards. He lives by his motto, You click only if you survive my lick.

Kamendra Singh Executive Chef

Award winning three-star Michelin chef with wide International experience having worked closely with whos-who in the culinary world, he specializes in creating mouthwatering Indo-Italian fusion experiences. He says, Put together the cuisines from the two craziest cultures, and you get a winning hit! Amma Mia!
